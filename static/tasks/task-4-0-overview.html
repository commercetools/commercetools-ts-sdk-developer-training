Manage Carts

Carts not only store the products a customer intends to purchase but also facilitates key actions like applying discounts and selecting shipping methods. Properly managing the cart is essential for driving sales and improving the customer journey. Lifestyle and Home Corp would like to implement that:

  • Customers can apply discount codes to their cart.
  • Customers can update their shipping address.

Steps


Perform the following programmatically, using the SDK:

  • Add a discount code to the cart.
  • Update shipping address on the cart.
