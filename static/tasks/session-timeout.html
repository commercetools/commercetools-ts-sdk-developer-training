Session Timed Out!



This simulation demonstrates the process of switching devices or logging out of your session. As a result, you will start as an unregistered customer. You can browse products and create a new anonymous cart. Later, when you sign in to your profile, you'll experience different cart merge options.

  • Visit Session 3: Create page to search for products and create a new cart.
  • Use the Login page to sign in with your user credentials and observe the changes in the cart.

By default, The current anonymous cart is merged with the most recently used customer cart.
